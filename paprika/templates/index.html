Paprika

A friendly order tracking tool.

Made with ♥ in Ann Arbor.

What does Paprika do?

Paprika allows your customers to follow along and stay up to date with their orders.

Why should I care?

Customers are curious and they want to feel like they are part of the process.

They will call (alot) in order to check in on their order.

These calls take time and when you're running a business these calls can become cumbersome.

Thats where Paprika comes in.

How does it work?

With Paprika you start by defining your flows.

Next you add an order.

As the order progresses, update its status.
